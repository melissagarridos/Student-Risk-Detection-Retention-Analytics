# Student Success Risk Analytics Dashboard

A business intelligence solution designed to identify students at risk of falling behind in workforce development programs through attendance, coaching engagement, and project completion metrics.

## Business Problem

Training programs often struggle to identify disengaged participants before performance declines significantly. Delayed intervention can result in lower completion rates, reduced program effectiveness, and increased dropout risk.

## Solution

This dashboard consolidates attendance records, Career Success Coach engagement, and project completion metrics into a unified risk-monitoring framework that enables proactive intervention strategies.

## Key Results

* Analyzed 216 fellows across the cohort.
* Identified 56 off-track students (25.9%).
* Discovered strong correlation between low coaching engagement and missed project submissions.
* Highlighted TA groups with higher concentrations of at-risk fellows.
* Enabled early intervention opportunities through KPI-based monitoring.

## Business Impact

The solution provides visibility into engagement trends and helps program managers prioritize interventions before performance deterioration becomes critical.
